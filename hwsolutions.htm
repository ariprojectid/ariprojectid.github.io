Hardware Solutions
Today, modern business operations depend heavily on reliable hardware. Ari Project provides complete hardware solutions to ensure your business systems run smoothly, efficiently, and without interruption.

Our Hardware Solutions include:

Hardware Upgrade (Memory & SSDs)
LCD/Monitor Replacement
Hardware Maintenance

  1. Hardware Upgrade
    • Installation and upgrade of RAM modules for faster system performance.
    • SSD upgrade and migration to boost boot speed and application load times.
    • Assessment of system requirements to recommend optimal upgrade paths.
    • Ensuring compatibility and stability for long-term use.
  2. LCD/Monitor Replacement
    • Replacement of damaged or malfunctioning LCD panels for laptops and desktops.
    • Support for various screen sizes, resolutions, and panel types.
    • Professional installation ensuring display accuracy and durability.
    • Troubleshooting display-related issues before recommending replacements.
  3. Hardware Maintenance
    • Routine cleaning and dust removal to prevent overheating.
    • Comprehensive diagnostics for hardware issues such as power, storage, and overheating problems.
    • Thermal paste replacement for improved cooling performance.
    • Preventive maintenance to extend hardware lifespan and reduce downtime.
